What is the Fiction Cabal?

The Fiction Cabal is a group of communities that have agreed to a partnership with our main Discord server.
Partnership Terms can be found below.

Partnership Agreement Terms

This is a binding agreement, failure to adhere to any part of the following text will lead to termination of partnership between our Discord Server and your community. To partner with our community, you must meet the following requirements:

  • At the time of partnership you must have a member count exceeding 29 seperate users, and excluding apps or bots.
  • The owner or a trusted admin of your community must join our Discord server. They will be given a designated role.
  • Your community must adhere to the discord terms of service and it's users must also adhere to the guidelines.
These are subject to change at any time. However previously partnershiped communities will be exempt from any changes regarding qualifying stats such as member count
